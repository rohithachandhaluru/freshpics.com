Cancellation Policy

At Fresh Picks, we understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel a booking or order, please review our cancellation policy below:

  • Cancellation Period: Cancellations can be made up to 48 hours before the scheduled visit or delivery date.
  • Refund Eligibility: If you cancel within the specified period, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on the circumstances.
  • No-shows: Failure to show up for a scheduled visit without prior notice may result in forfeiture of any refund.
