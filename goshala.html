Benefits of Indigenous Cows

Healthy Milk: Indigenous Indian cow milk is sattvic and nutritious. It contains vitamin A2, B, D, E, K, minerals, amino acids, salts, and enzymes in an easily digestible form.

Cow Dung: Cow dung is a valuable natural fertilizer and pesticide for farming. It's also used as fuel and for biogas production. Fresh cow dung has antibacterial properties.

Useful Urine: Distilled cow urine (gomutra) is used as medicine for various diseases. It contains essential nutrients and minerals, including iron, calcium, and phosphorus. Cow urine is also used in making medicines.
